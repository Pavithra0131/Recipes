Stuffed brinjal-Ennegayi palya

Ennegayi or stuffed brinjal recipe explained with step by step pictures and a video. Badanekayi ennegayi or thumbida badanekayi is a very tasty and extremely popular curry recipe from North Karnataka. Ennegayi is mainly served with jowar roti (jolada rotti) or chapathi. Tasty ennegayi or stuffed brinjal is prepared using small purpole brinjals, onion, coconut, peanut, sesame seeds and many other spices. In Kannada this curry is familiar by name ennegayi or thumbida badanekayi. "Enne" translates to oil in Kannada language. Since brinjal or badanekayi is cooked under low flame in oil, it is called by name ennegayi. Also "thumbida" translates to stuffed in Kannada language. And so comes the name thumbida badanekayi or stuffed brinjal.

instructions

step 1

Wash and slit the brinjals in '+' sign retaining the stalk portion. Please refer the image. Also check for worms or any spoiled part.cutting brinjals for ennegayi recipe Now immersse the slit brinjals in water for 10 minutes.immersing brinjals for ennegayi recipe Meantime let us start roasting the ingredients for grinding. Take a frying pan and dry roast sesame seeds until they start spluttering. Once it is done take it out and keep it aside.roasting sesame seeds for brinjals for ennegayi recipe Next in the same pan dry roast ground nuts or peanuts until slightly brown. Once it is done take them out and keep it aside.roasting peanuts for ennegayi recipe Next in the same pan add in a tsp of oil, 3 red chilies, 2 tsp gram dal, 1 tsp urad dal, 2 tsp coriander seeds, 1 tsp cumin seeds and 1/4 fenugreek seeds. Fry them under medium flame until dals are brown. Take care not to burn anything.roasting spices for ennegayi recipe

step 2

Once done take them out and keep it aside. Now have a look at all the ingredients roasted. Add in fried gram and grated coconut.roasting spices for ennegayi recipe Transfer all the roasted ingredients, fried gram and coconut into a mixie jar and grind it into a thick paste by adding required water. Keep it aside.grinding roasted spices for ennegayi recipe Now take 4 tbsp of oil in the same pan and heat it. Add in mustard seeds. When the mustard seeds splutters add in curry leaves and chopped onions.tempering for ennegayi recipe Fry the onions until soft. Then add in turmeric powder. Give a quick stir and reduce the flame.turmeric powder for ennegayi recipe Now take out the brinjals from the water. Remove the excess water. And then stuff the ground masala inside the slit brinjals.stuffing brinjals for ennegayi recipe

step 3

Place the stuffed brinjals in the frying pan which has onion tempering.cooking brinjals for ennegayi recipe After few minutes turn the brinjals and cook the remaining sides under low flame.cooking brinjals for ennegayi recipe Add in tamarind juice extracted from a gooseberry sized tamarind, jaggery and salt.tamarind and salt for ennegayi recipe Continue cooking the brinjals for few more minutes. Keep turning the brinjals and cook on all sides under low flame.cooking brinjals for ennegayi recipe Once the brinjals are soft add in remaining masala and 1 cup of water. Cover and cook the brinjals for few more minutes.masala and water for ennegayi recipe After around 5 minutes or when the masala starts leaving the oil it is done. Sprinkle the chopped coriander leaves. Serve it with hot rice flour roti or jowar roti or chapathi.

ingredients

5 - 6 small tender purple brinjals

1 big onion

1 gooseberry sized tamarind

2 tsp jaggery

1/4 tsp turmeric powder

4 - 5 curry leaves

1 tbsp finely chopped coriander leaves

4 tbsp cooking oil

Salt as per your taste

Ingredients for grinding:
2 tsp sesame seeds
2 tbsp ground nuts or peanuts
3 - 6 red chilies
2 tsp coriander seeds
1 tsp cumin seeds
2 tsp gram dal or chana dal
1 tsp urad dal
1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
2 tsp roasted gram or fried gram
1/2 cup grated coconut
